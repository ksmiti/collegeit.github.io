From The Desk of Manager

It is the matter of immense happiness for me to taking over the responsibilities of principal. During my Career of long service in educational industries, I found this institute more emphasis on practical training.

Educational Institutions are a plenty, all of which impart a learning, but it is our prayer that our students go out into the world with the strength, not only to reach and excel in their professional aspirations, but to remain beautiful human beings.

Sant Ramakrishna Private Audyogik Prashikshan Sansthan iti is always a 'world in miniature' where one receives a 'training for life', where effective, meaningful and joyful learning takes place.  About Sant Ramakrishna Private Audyogik Prashikshan Sansthan. ITI


The Sant Ramakrishna Private Audyogik Prashikshan Sansthan. ITI, is located in Manoharpur Balkeshwar Road. Agra(U.P.) , the District Head Quarters.Established in the Year 2014. It has been set-up under the Craftsman Training Scheme affiliated to National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT), through the Directorate General of Employment and Training (DGE&T), Govt. of India under the ministry of Labour, New Delhi.The following Technical courses below were taught with hand - on training Principal Trades :

  • Electrician
  • Fitter
  • Tailoring and Sewing
 SALIENT FEATURES
  • Afflilated By NCVT.
  • Decent Administrative Buildings and Workshops with Modern Equipment.
  • Special care should be taken to fill up the form without over writing
  • Theory Class Rooms with Projector and White Boards.
  • Linkage with Local Industry
  • Providing Apprenticeship to the Passed Trainees in Various Industries and Establishments.
  • Library with more than 200 Technical Books.
  • Offers scholarships for SC/ST/OBC candidates.
