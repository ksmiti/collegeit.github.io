Chairman Message

The Institute is Established in the rural heart of Agra with a vision providing value based technical education to the local students with a valid certificate to enable them to get employment in Indian/abroad Industries/ to continue studies for higher diploma/degrees as per their financial capabilities and choice.
Neeraj Yadav (Chairman)

 About Karan Singh Memorial ITI



We at KARAN SINGH MEMORIAL I.T.I have the Mission to impart technical education to the students of most rural segment of District Agra so that they can aware of the importance and value of technical education in present era of life .
Besides this Rural area Institute will provide technical knowledge with valid certification to get employment in the Industries any where in India or abroad:

  • Electrician
 SALIENT FEATURES
  • Decent Administrative Buildings and Workshops with Modern Equipment.
  • Special care should be taken to fill up the form without over writing
  • Theory Class Rooms with Projector and White Boards.
  • Linkage with Local Industry
  • Providing Apprenticeship to the Passed Trainees in Various Industries and Establishments.
  • Library with more than 200 Technical Books.
  • Offers scholarships for SC/ST/OBC candidates.
